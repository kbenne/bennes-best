About Our Farm

For generations, the Benne family has been committed to sustainable farming practices in St. Charles County. What began as a traditional family farm has evolved into a pillar of sustainability in our community.

At Benne's Best, we believe in farming with purpose. Our animals are:

  • Free-range and humanely raised in natural environments
  • Never given antibiotics or growth hormones
  • Fed with custom ground feed from locally sourced grains
  • Raised using sustainable farming practices

We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare while producing exceptional quality meats. By selling directly to our customers, we preserve the connection between farmer and consumer that has been lost in modern agriculture.

Our farm stands as a testament to the viability of small-scale, sustainable agriculture even as suburban development encroaches. We invite you to taste the difference that comes from meat raised with care and respect for both the animals and the land.
